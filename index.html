Mathura – Vrindavan

The pious city of Mathura is the epicenter of BrajBhoomi and is well known worldwide as the birthplace of the Karma Yogi Lord Krishna. It is well connected to various parts of the country by rail and road. It is 120 km. from Delhi, the capital city of India. The famous city of “Tajmahal”, Agra is 70 km. from Mathura.

Uttar pradesh's Best University.

GLA University is one of the premier universities in India, situated in Northern India. The university was established by our present Chancellor, Shri Narayan Das Agrawal in 1998. About Us

In 1991, Shri Narayan Das Agrawal decided to fulfill his father's dream, the Late Shri Ganeshi Lal Agrawal, of establishing an institute for quality education for the people and the region & beyond. And initiated Sri Jagannath Prasad Ganeshi Lal Bazaz Charitable Trust Samiti to make the holy city Mathura a recognized destination for knowledge seekers from different spheres of life. This led to the foundation of a milestone at the karmabhoomi of the versatile and sagacious Lord Krishna.
